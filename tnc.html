Terms and Conditions

For Creative Content Contributor

  • Creative Content must be a personal work and the contributor must own the copyright upon everything within it.
  • The Captured Image must be of 1280 x 720 resolution or above.
  • All pictures in this domain will be sold at a fixed price.
  • Color scheme must be RGB, rendered at the highest JPG|JPEG quality (100%).
  • The contributor must have a Model Release for all recognizable people in their image.(Include this link in anchor tag https://digital-photography-school.com/model-releases-need-know/)
  • In case of traced Illustrations contributor needs to hold copyright upon the original image as well.
  • Check carefully for copyright issues such as labels, logos, cartoons character, brands, storefronts, building under trademarks etc.
  • We don't accept offensive content such as explicit nudes, drugs, people in offensive positions etc.
  • Model/Property release files must be JPEG, filenames must contain only letters and numbers, no special characters.
  • Subjects of Interest include: Models, cultural diversity, architecture, beauty, business, technology, fashion, food, portraiture, lifestyle, more(add an anchor tag to the category tab in the navbar.)

About Glowing Pixels

The world we dwell in is always better when captured in a frame of pixels. In the world of pixels, we filter out all the negatives and malice, only to treasure the moments of pure emotions and joys with our lenses. Our organization works as an intermediary trying to bring together the different photographers and art connoisseurs together. We are thus bridging the Gap between good insightful people who like to see every picture with a different eye.
